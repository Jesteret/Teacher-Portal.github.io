How to Play Potato Chip Clicker

Welcome to Potato Chip Clicker, the ultimate potato chip clicking experience! Follow these simple steps to become a Potato Chip GOD:

  1. Click the Potato Chip: The more you click, the more potato chips you earn.
  2. Unlock Achievements: Clicking will unlock various achievements. Check your progress on the achievements bar.
  3. Explore Achievements: There are 50 achievements to unlock. Can you collect them all and become the Potato Chip God?
  4. Or Reach 1M Potato Chips: The Goal is 1M Potato Chips, Just reach 1M Potato Chips and you win.

Click the "Play" button to start your potato chip clicking adventure. Have fun!
